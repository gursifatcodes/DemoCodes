Why Adoption

is a best choice?

Overburdened shelters take in millions of stray, abused and lost animals every year, and by adopting an animal, you're making room for others. Not only are you giving more animals a chance to live a better life, but the cost of your donation goes directly towards helping those shelters better care for the animals they take in!
